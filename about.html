Who We Are

We are a premier palm vein biometric solution committed to improving how people use and interact with their identities for various applications. Our technology uses palm vein patterns to enhance security in various identity and authentication domains. Follow us through this journey, learn about our mission, and discover the products already making this change possible.

Our Mission

Our mission is straightforward: to provide secure, convenient, and reliable palm vein biometric and authentication solutions that empower individuals and organizations worldwide. We use the latest technologies and tools to make this possible, streamlining the process and improving user experiences across various industries that need secure authentication methods.

Our Commitment to Security and Data Privacy

At Cetirc, we understand the importance of security and data privacy and how it affects our brand and those who trust us. This is why we adhere to strict industry standards and best practices to protect user data and palm vein signatures.

We are committed to maintaining solid integrity, transparency, and accountability in all our operation aspects. Our products—Alice 1 and Alice 2—have robust encryption mechanisms for securely storing our users’ palm signatures. We also perform security audits regularly to reduce risks and vulnerabilities.

Our Story

We’ve not slacked in our vision of developing advanced biometric solutions that address the rising identification and digital signature problems and inefficiencies. More importantly, we've made giant strides in research, development, implementation, and partnership to establish ourselves as a trusted leader in the biometrics industry.
