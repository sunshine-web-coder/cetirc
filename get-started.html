Getting Started With Alice 1 and Alice 2

Cetirc welcomes you to the world of palm vein biometrics with Alice 1 and Alice 2. We've got you covered whether you're seeking secure payment solutions or advanced access control and identification services.

This guide will walk you through getting started with Alice 1 and Alice 2, their features, and our security and privacy assurance when using the Cetirc palm vein biometrics products.

Overview of Alice 1 and Alice 2

  • Alice 1 and Alice 2 are cutting-edge palm vein identification services developed by Cetirc. Both products use palm vein biometrics, one of today's most secure and accurate biometric authentication methods.

  • To use either Alice 1 or Alice 2 offerings, you must register with the Alice 1 app or offline using our Cetirc biometrics device. The app is free to download, sign up/register, and easy to use.

  • Apart from onboarding and identification registry, you can use Alice 1 for secure palm vein payment services at POS and retail stores or outlets.

  • Alice 2 specifically offers advanced access control and identification capabilities. With Alice 2 you can gain entrance into any department, door, or center partnering with Cetirc.

  • The Alice 1 hardware comes at a cost for enterprise/business use. Also, Alice 2 access control and identification come at a cost. However, we offer them at competitive prices.

Why Use Palm Vein Biometrics?

Our palm vein biometrics offer several advantages over traditional authentication methods, such as passwords, PINs, or fingerprint scans.

  • High Security:

    Palm vein patterns are unique to each individual and virtually impossible to replicate. This uniqueness provides a high level of security against identity theft and fraud.

  • Contactless:

    Unlike fingerprint or iris scans, palm vein authentication is non-invasive and does not require physical contact, which is more hygienic and convenient.

  • Accurate and Reliable:

    Palm vein identification technology is invulnerable to compromising environmental factors like dirt, moisture, temperature changes, etc., and boasts high accuracy and reliability rates.

How Does Alice 1 Work?

Alice 1 improves the retail and POS payment experiences with palm vein biometrics. Here's how it works:

  • Registration:

    You register your palm vein patterns using the Alice 1 app or through Cetirc-supported devices at designated registration points. Our app or device’s proprietary computer vision algorithm captures and securely stores your unique palm vein patterns during registration. This system encrypts your palm image and creates your unique palm signature, readable by any of our partnering Cetirc or Alice 1 devices worldwide.

  • Payment:

    After registration, you can make payments at partnering merchants, retail stores, or centers by simply scanning your palm at the point of sale terminal. The system compares your palm image with the palm signatures in our system to authenticate the transaction securely and swiftly.

  • What’s More?

    You unlock access to Alice 2 offerings upon completing registration with Alice 1 via the app or registration point. We intentionally made this possible to provide a seamless and integrated user experience.

How Does Alice 2 Work?

Alice 2 uses palm vein biometrics technology to authenticate access control and identification activities.

What Does It Do?

  • Access Control:

    Alice 2 enables secure access control to restricted areas or facilities by authenticating users based on their palm signature created with Alice 1. Employees or authorized personnel can enter by scanning their palms at the designated access control points.

  • Identification:

    Beyond access control, Alice 2 offers identity verification in various scenarios, such as attendance tracking, visitor management, or secure document access. Users can quickly, accurately, and conveniently confirm their identity by scanning their palm vein pattern.

  • Integration:

    By adopting and integrating Alice 2, small and large businesses, enterprises, and organizations can improve and scale their existing security systems and protocols.

Security and Data Privacy on Alice 1 and Alice 2

At Cetirc, we prioritize your security and data privacy and ensure that Alice 1 and Alice 2 adhere to uncompromising security standards. Additionally, both products employ robust encryption techniques to safeguard user data and palm signatures.

For more context, access to sensitive information is strictly controlled and monitored to prevent unauthorized access or misuse. In fact, you control when and where to authenticate payments and access control with your palm signature.
