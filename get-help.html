Signing Up or Joining Cetirc

Registering Your Palm ID With Alice 1 (App and Device)

Before joining the cetirc ecosystem or using any of our products, you must register your profile on Alice 1. We have an app/software which is more convenient and discreet. Meanwhile, you could register with our palm ID device at approved centers or partners.

When registering on the Alice 1 (app or device), you must provide details about your identity and location. These requirements are crucial for complying with federal and local regulations regarding age, accredited countries, accepted partners, AML policies, etc., across countries or jurisdictions that use Cetirc palm vein ID services. This ensures the security and integrity of our platform, making your experience with Cetirc safe and reliable.

Completing KYC

Know your customer (KYC) is compulsory for regulatory purposes. Information requested during KYC may include full name, date of birth, ID type (driver's license, government ID, voters card, etc.), ID number and the organization or jurisdiction issuing it, country of origin (including state/province/LGA), and the ID expiration date. We will also ask you to take a selfie to confirm the relevance of this information to you.

Keep in mind that you may be denied access when using products whose policies contradict the information provided during registration. For example, suppose a product of one of our partners prohibits people of certain ages from processing transactions or accessing certain areas or resources through Alice 2. In that case, you won’t be allowed access.

Performing Transactions and Payments

Alice 1 allows you to perform transactions or process payments at POS units partnering with Cetirc. We are still growing and expanding worldwide, so you must search for locations or commercial centers that use Alice 1 for POS payments or clearance

After confirming if your shopping choice partners with us, you can scan your palm at the point of payment to authenticate transactions from your debit or credit card. This is used in place of PINs or passwords. Still, Alice 1 supports most credit cards, including NPC, PINless, and PIN types. However, it doesn’t support prepaid and gift cards.

Data Privacy and Security

What Happens During Registration?

During registration, you’re asked to capture your palm biometrically. This image is translated to create your unique palm signature and then deleted. Cetirc or Alice 1 does not use this image to identify you anywhere but for select services or activities requiring your approval or authentication. We neither share your data with third parties nor use it for purposes other than creating a better Cetirc experience for our users.

What Happens After Registration?

Cetirc solely uses the palm image you provide to create your unique palm signature. This signature is your identity, indicating you’re a bonafide Cetirc product user or partner worldwide. So, when you visit any accredited commercial hub or store partnering with Cetirc, you can authenticate activities or transactions using this unique signature created on Alice 1 or Cetirc.

How Long Do We Keep Your Data?

If you are curious about how long we keep your palm data, rest assured that they are removed from our database or the database of our partners or enterprise clients when you discontinue using Alice 1, Alice 2, or any other Cetirc palm vein technology.

Cetirc is behind the palm vein biometric products Alice 1 and Alice 2. We are currently focused on building these products, growing our users, and partnering with more enterprises and organizations worldwide.

Q2. What Is Alice 1, and How Does It Work?

Alice 1 is a proprietary palm vein biometric technology designed by Cetirc. Its primary use case is to authenticate payments at retail outlets or POS units. Additionally, you can use Alice 1 to join the Cetirc ecosystem and gain access to other up-and-coming products, including Alice 2.

Alice 1 is available as both software and hardware. You may use either of both options to register your palm signature in our database. Download Alice 1 on the Play Store or App Store to get started. Download App

Q3. What Is Alice 2, and How Does It Work?

Alice 2 is a proprietary technology that uses palm vein technology for access control and identification. The Alice 2 software and algorithm apply to entrances or exit units.

To use Alice 2, you must first register your profile and palm vein signature on Alice 2 via the app or device. Meanwhile, enterprise users may request custom registration methods just like using the Alice 1 app and registering their palm data on a database.

Q4. Are Cetirc Palm Vein ID Products Available to Everyone?

We are still expanding our reach and user base. To be clear, we may not accept all regions due to the wavering and fluctuating data privacy and security policies. Read our data privacy disclosure to learn more.

Q5. Is Cetirc the Same As Alice 1 or Alice 2?

Cetirc is the parent organization responsible for managing, maintaining, updating, and exercising legal authority over Alice 1 and Alice 2.
